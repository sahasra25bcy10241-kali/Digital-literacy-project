Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist:


1. Never share your UPI PIN with anyone
2. Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
3. Always verify the source before making payments
4. Do not approve payment requests from unknown contacts
5. Use strong and unique passwords
6. Enable two-factor authentication where possible
7. Regularly check your bank transactions
8. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
